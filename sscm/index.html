About the Collective

Island roots,
global reach.

Salt Spring Collective Media is a rotating group of six to ten artisans, media creators, designers, and industry professionals based on Salt Spring Island, British Columbia.

We come together around projects that need more than one perspective — bringing decades of individual experience into a single, responsive team.

Creative Direction
