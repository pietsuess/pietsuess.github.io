About
I've always been a creative director.

I started making films at 14 and have never stopped. New York, Los Angeles, internationally — 30+ years leading creative teams and setting the vision
for work that lasts.

The people I work with become partners, not clients — and most of them come back. Collaborations that started as projects turned into friendships, and those friendships keep producing work — over years, over decades.
