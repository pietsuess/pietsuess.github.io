About
I've been making things
since I was fourteen.

When I was 14, I started spending every day and night in a basement with two VCRs. Tape-to-tape editing — linear and destructive — learning to feel where a cut should land. That drive just kept building — into directing, editing, photography, animation, and brand design. New York, Los Angeles, internationally — 30+ years of mastering every discipline the work asked for.

I stay until it's right. The people I work with become partners, not clients — and most of them come back. Collaborations that started as projects turned into friendships, and those friendships keep producing work — over years, over decades. The craft and the people go hand in hand. You can't sacrifice either.
