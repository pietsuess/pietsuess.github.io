Live commerce that actually feels like community. That’s a brand worth building from the inside out. The energy in those streams, the passion the sellers bring, the way a sneaker drop or a card break can turn a Tuesday night into an event. That’s the stuff I love working with.

Building identity systems from scratch is what gets me giddy. Van Solo, Convergence Festival, Chasm Audio, Hayan Collection. Each one started as a conversation and became a complete world. Logos, typography, packaging, the whole thing. Taking someone’s passion and giving it a face that feels exactly right. The challenge of translating energy across physical and digital surfaces is a welcome one, and it never gets old.

Every discipline the work has put in front of me became the next one. Editing led to directing. Directing led to brand design. Brand design led to campaign systems, experiential work, merchandise, social content. Over thirty-five years of saying yes to whatever the project needed. 80+ credits across narrative, documentary, music video, branded content, and commercial.

At Maximum Effort, Ryan Reynolds’ name was on everything, which meant the bar was taste. Always. Fast, relentless, and fun. That team genuinely loved the work and you could feel it in every cut. Forbes’ #1 Best Startup Employer sounds like a place with that same energy.

Since childhood, cracking open creation tools has been the constant. Painting pixels in MS-DOS Paintbrush, editing the source code of Gorillas and Nibbles to make them do things they weren’t supposed to. That instinct hasn’t changed, rather has grown with skill and experience. Always the first one in. First to learn the tool, first to break it, first to find what it’s actually good for. These new AI tools are force multipliers, not magic wands. Taste will always prevail.

The thing that comes most naturally is leading teams. Finding people’s greatest strengths and pushing them to go further. Play lives inside the work, and that energy is contagious. People want to be part of it. The people who collaborate with me become partners, and most of them come back. What started as projects turned into friendships, and those friendships keep producing work.

Wrapping a brand around all of that community energy, across every category and every activation, would be a delightful undertaking.

US citizen based in British Columbia, experienced working remotely across time zones.
