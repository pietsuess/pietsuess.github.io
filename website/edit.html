Editing

When I was 14 I began my obsession with post by spending countless days and nights in a basement with two VCR's. I was tirelessly compiling short films and school projects made with my friends. Kung-Fu movies, Sci-fi epics and Documentaries about my family. The tape to tape process was linear and destructive, and required precise timing to make an edit.

That was 1995 and since then the software and my abilities have come a long way. Fortunately the techniques I learned in those days are still as relevant.

Over 30 years of experience in editing and post production. DaVinci Resolve / Premiere Pro / Avid / After Effects / Blender / GSD / A.I.
