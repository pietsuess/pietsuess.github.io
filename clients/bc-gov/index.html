Approach — How I Work
01
Philosophy
  • Partnership, not transactions
  • Understand before designing
  • Clarity over decoration
Principle 01Listen First
  • Every project starts with understanding — who's it for, what does it need to do, what's the context
  • I don't design until I understand the problem well enough to explain it back to you
Principle 02One Person, Every Discipline
  • I design, animate, edit, and build — all in-house, one point of contact
  • No handoffs between departments means nothing gets lost in translation
  • A brochure, a motion graphic, and a tradeshow banner all feel like they came from the same hand — because they did
Principle 03Accessible by Default
  • Proper reading order, tagged PDFs, accessible link formatting, alt text — built in, not bolted on
  • Source documents (Word, PowerPoint, InDesign) structured for accessibility from the start
Principle 04Offsite, Always Available
  • Based in British Columbia. Working remotely for over a decade with clients across North America
  • Responsive, reliable, and used to government timelines and approval processes
