Phase 01 — Logo & Brand
01
What we'll explore
  • 1–2 text mark logo concepts
  • 1–2 icon logo concepts
  • Refined final logo files
Step 01Getting to Know Lotus
  • A questionnaire to get the lay of the land — who you are, where you're headed, who you're up against
  • A 45min–1hr call where we talk it all through and get on the same page
Step 02Look & Feel
  • I'll pull together a visual direction specific to Lotus — mood, references, examples
  • We'll go back and forth on what excites us and what doesn't
  • By the end of this we'll have landed on the flavour, the tone, the visual voice
Step 03Locking Direction
  • We solidify what the logo needs to say and how it needs to feel
  • No design begins until we're both excited about where it's going
Step 04Presentation
  • 1–2 text mark concepts, each with the thinking behind it
  • 1–2 icon concepts — same deal, nothing presented without rationale
Phase 02 — Brand Identity & Guidelines
02
What you'll own
  • Full brand guidelines document
  • Colour palette, type system & supporting graphics
  • All logo files — print & digital
Step 01Refinement
  • Take your feedback from Phase 1 and work it into the chosen direction
  • Finalise the logo so it feels right in your hands
Step 02Building the System
  • Colours, fonts, supporting graphic elements — the full palette
  • These are the pieces that make Lotus feel like Lotus everywhere it shows up
Step 03The Rules
  • How to use the logo, how not to — so it always looks its best
  • Spacing, sizing, placement — the details that keep everything clean
Step 04Hand Off
  • The complete guidelines presented with every element explained
  • All assets organised, labelled, and ready to use
Phase 03 — Packaging
03
What we'll build
  • 1–2 full packaging concepts
  • All panel layouts — print-ready
  • Dieline files & production specs
Step 01Getting Into It
  • What are we working with — size, materials, print methods, what the shelf looks like
  • I want to understand the product the way you understand it
Step 02Look & Feel
  • Develop the packaging direction rooted in everything we built in Phase 1 & 2
  • References, mood, what catches the eye on a shelf and what gets lost
Step 03Locking Direction
  • Nail down the hierarchy — what's big, what's small, where the eye goes first
  • Agree on the layout approach for every panel before I start designing
Step 04Presentation
  • 1–2 full packaging concepts with every panel thought through
  • Realistic 3D mockups so you can feel it before it's printed
Phase 04 — Final Files & Support
04
What you'll walk away with
  • Final approved files — everything you need
  • Print-ready & digital formats
  • Post-launch support
Step 01Last Looks
  • We go through everything together one final time
  • Make sure every file is exactly where it needs to be
Step 02File Prep
  • Finalise all files for sign-off
  • Print-ready, web, digital — every format you'll need
Step 03Delivery
  • Everything packaged up, clearly named, and organised
  • No hunting around — you'll know exactly where everything lives
Step 04I'm Still Here
  • 1–2 minor tweaks after you start using everything in the real world
  • Questions, guidance, whatever comes up — I'm around
