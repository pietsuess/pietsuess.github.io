The Space

Loft Living at Its Finest

Perched on the top floor of MainSpace with no one above you, this rare sub-penthouse is a true loft in every sense. Soaring 10-foot-plus ceilings open up beneath a massive skylight that floods the space with natural light throughout the day. Full-height windows frame private views of the city skyline and the North Shore mountains beyond.

The open-concept layout features polished concrete floors, excellent built-ins, and the kind of flexible, creative space that Mount Pleasant is known for. Whether you work from home, entertain, or simply want room to breathe, this is a unit that adapts to your life, not the other way around.
